About The Foundation


Amanda Fields

Veterinarian/Co-Founder

Known as the Owl Whisperer. Dr. Amanda Fields is a leading veterinarian specializing in WORD FOR BIRD STUDIES. Co-Founder at Give a Hoot and primary caregiver of our feathered friends, she has worked with owls for over a decade and dedicates her time to helping sick, injured or displaced owls getting them healthy again and ready to be released back into their natural habitat. Her daily activities include caring for our birds that live on-site ensuring they are living a happy life here with us at Give a Hoot.


Genise Butler

CEO/Co-Founder

The brains of the operation. Co-Founder and CEO of The Give a Hoot Foundation, Genise uses her knowledge and years of business management experience to navigate and spread our mission across the country. Behind the scenes is where the magic happens and her networking and determination makes it possible for our organization to thrive. She uses her skills and education daily to drive our foundation forward towards our goal. Genise loves to visit the sanctuary and enjoys learning as much as she can about the birds for Dr. Fields.


Josephine Matteucci

Director/Co-Founder

Owl enthusiast. Co-Founder and Director at Give a Hoot she uses her love of these birds to motivate her to get things done. She has loved owls since she was a little girl and turned that love into a mission to save them. Her role in the organization includes fundraising, networking, educating other on owls and what we can all do to help them. She loves spending time with the owls in our on-site forever home and finds the work both educational and rewarding


Marlow Ross

Public Relations/Co-Founder

Truly gives a hoot. Co-Founder and head of Public Relations Marlow's role plays a huge part in our organizations partnerships. She puts a face to our name and meets with land owners, companies and others who are interested in helping us help owls. Using social media and any other available outlet she utilizes every resource to spread our message across the country.
