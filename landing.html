Our Mission?

Owls. We Care About Them.

We want to save as many as we can. Over time due to land development and environmental changes, owls have been running out of land and some of the species have even become endangered. Here at Give a Hoot we want to help. A hardworking and dedicated team of owl lovers, we come together everyday to rescue sick, injured or displaced birds. Whether they get released back into their natural habitat or find a forever home here with us, donations and partnerships make it possible for Give a Hoot to ensure these owls get the care they need to live longer and happier lives.
